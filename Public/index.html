01

Circle Ledger

A simple way to track contributions in njangi, savings groups, and any group money activity

Problem

In most njangi houses, savings groups, and school clubs, one person keeps the contribution records. When people contribute at different times, updates are delayed, mistakes happen, and when issues come up, there is no clear record everyone can trust.

Solution

Circle Ledger is a shared digital record where contributions are entered once and seen by the whole group. Instead of depending on one book or one person, everyone can check the same updated record at any time.

Benefit

It reduces confusion, builds trust, and saves time for both members and group leaders. It only records contributions. It does not collect or move money.

02

BaseDeck

Manage your Supabase backend directly from your phone

Problem

Many backend tools are built for laptops. When you are using only your phone or away from your system, it becomes difficult to fix issues, check data, or deploy updates quickly.

Solution

BaseDeck is a mobile-first tool that connects to your Supabase project and lets you manage your backend from your browser. You can run queries, view tables, and handle backend tasks without needing a laptop.

Benefit

You are no longer blocked when something breaks. You can access and manage your backend anytime, from anywhere, using just your phone.

03

Quick Wa Link

Create WhatsApp chat links with ready messages without stress

Problem

When running promotions or sharing contact links, creating a correct WhatsApp link with message and country code is not straightforward. Small mistakes can break the link or confuse users.

Solution

Quick Wa Link is a simple tool that generates a ready-to-use WhatsApp link from a phone number and message. It handles the formatting in the background so the link works correctly.

Benefit

It saves time and removes errors when sharing WhatsApp links. You focus on your campaign while the tool handles the formatting.
